# AI SEO Glossary of Key Terms 
 
A reference document compiled during the research phase. 
 
## AI Overviews 
Google's AI-generated summary boxes at the top of search results. Reduces click-through rates on informational queries by 20-30%. 
 
## E-E-A-T 
Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness. Google's framework for evaluating content quality. The first E (Experience) is hardest for pure AI content to demonstrate. 
 
## Topical Authority 
The concept of a website covering a subject so comprehensively that Google treats it as a trusted source. Built through content clusters, not individual pages. 
 
## Semantic SEO 
An approach focused on entities, relationships, and meaning rather than keyword frequency. Requires mapping topics to knowledge graph entities. 
 
## Programmatic SEO 
Using templates and databases to generate thousands of pages targeting long-tail keywords at scale. AI has dramatically lowered the cost of this approach. 
 
## Helpful Content System 
Google's algorithmic classifier designed to reward content made for humans first. Sites with high proportions of AI-only content have been penalized by this system.
